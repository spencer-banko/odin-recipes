Spaghetti Yum!

Follow these instructions carefully and you'll have the best spaghetti you've ever tried!

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 jar tomato sauce
  • 1 bag of pasta of your choice
  • grated parmesean cheese about 1.5 cups
  • 2 tbsp of melted butter
  • 1 tbsp of oregano

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Heat water on stove until boiling
  2. Add pasta into water once boiling
  3. Let pasta cook for 7 minutes
  4. Strain pasta in strainer
  5. Pour pasta into bowl and combine with cheese, sauce, and melted butter
  6. Stir in oregano and other seasonings of choice.
