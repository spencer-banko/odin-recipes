Cesar Salad Yippee

Follow these instructions carefully and you'll have the best cesar salad you've ever tried! There is no excuse not to be healthy.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 head of romaine lettuce
  • block of parmesean cheese
  • 1 bottle of cesar dressing
  • black pepper

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Wash, rinse, and dry your lettuce
  2. Cut lettuce into 1 inch long sections
  3. In a large bowl, mix the lettuce, dressing, parmesean, and black pepper together
